## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Download & Open Fluzio**

### Mission Types

#### 1. Social Media Missions
**Post Content:**
- Create an Instagram post, story, or reel
- Include required hashtags (e.g., #FluzioPartner #[BusinessName])
- Mention the business account (e.g., @BusinessHandle)
- Submit a screenshot or link as proof

**Example:**

```
- Post an Instagram story
- Tag @CafeFluzio

```

#### 2. Visit & Review Missions
**In-Person Visits:**
- Visit the business location
- Make a purchase (if required)
- Take a photo or get a receipt
- Submit proof of visit

#### 3. Engagement Missions
**Social Engagement:**
- Follow business on social media
- Share their content
- Write a review
- Invite friends

### Step-by-Step: Completing a Mission

1. **Browse Missions**
   - Tap on a mission card to view details
   - Read requirements carefully
   - Check points reward and deadline

2. **Apply for Mission**
   - Tap "Apply Now" button
   - Confirm you can complete requirements
   - Mission appears in "My Missions" tab

3. **Complete Requirements**
   - Follow mission instructions exactly
   - Create content (if social media mission)
   - Visit location (if in-person mission)
   - Gather proof (screenshot, photo, receipt)

4. **Submit Proof**
   - Go to "My Missions" ‚Üí "Active"
   - Tap on the mission
   - Tap "Submit Proof"
   - Upload screenshot or photo
   - Add any notes (optional)
   - Tap "Submit for Review"

5. **Wait for Approval**
   - Business reviews your submission (usually 24-48 hours)
   - You'll receive a notification when reviewed
   - Points are automatically added to your wallet

## Redeeming Rewards

### How to Redeem

1. **Browse Rewards**
   - Go to "Wallet" tab
   - Tap "Browse Rewards" or visit business profiles
   - View available rewards from businesses

2. **Choose Your Reward**
   - Select a reward you want
   - Check points cost and requirements
   - Read redemption instructions

3. **Redeem Points**
   - Tap "Redeem Now"
   - Confirm points deduction
   - Receive redemption code or QR code

4. **Use Your Reward**
   - Show QR code at the business
   - OR use the redemption code online
   - Business will mark it as used

### Reward Types

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Discount** | % off your purchase | 20% off entire order |
| **Free Item** | Complimentary product | Free coffee or dessert |
| **Experience** | Exclusive access | VIP event invitation |
| **Service** | Complimentary service | Free consultation |

### Redemption Tips

‚úÖ **Check expiration dates** - Some rewards expire after 30 days  
‚úÖ **Read terms & conditions** - May have restrictions (min. purchase, etc.)  
‚úÖ **Show QR code clearly** - Make sure it scans easily  
‚úÖ **Ask staff if unsure** - Business staff can help with redemption

### Refund Policy

If a mission is cancelled by the business or rejected unfairly:
- **Full refund** - If business cancels before completion
- **Partial refund** - If rejected after good-faith effort (admin review)
- Points are automatically returned to your wallet

## Troubleshooting

### Mission Issues

**Q: My mission was rejected. Why?**

Common reasons:
- ‚ùå Missing required hashtags
- ‚ùå Didn't mention business account
- ‚ùå Photo doesn't show required content
- ‚ùå Submitted after deadline
- ‚ùå Content doesn't match requirements

**Solution:** Read rejection reason, fix issues, resubmit if allowed.

---

**Q: I completed a mission but it's not showing in my history.**

**Solution:**
1. Check "My Missions" ‚Üí "In Review" tab
2. Ensure you submitted proof (not just applied)
3. Wait 24-48 hours for review
4. Contact support if still missing after 48 hours

---

**Q: The business hasn't reviewed my mission for 3+ days.**

**Solution:**
1. Check if business is still active
2. Send a polite message to business via app
3. Contact Fluzio support if no response after 5 days
4. May be eligible for automatic approval or refund

---

### Points Issues

**Q: My points balance is incorrect.**

**Solution:**
1. Check "Transaction History" for all activity
2. Look for pending points (missions in review)
3. Check for refunds (cancelled missions)
4. Contact support with specific transaction details

---

**Q: Points were deducted but reward didn't work.**

**Solution:**
1. Check "My Rewards" for redemption code
2. Verify reward hasn't expired
3. Try showing QR code to business staff
4. Contact support for refund if reward was invalid

---

### Instagram Connection Issues

**Q: Instagram won't connect.**

**Error Types & Solutions:**

**"Authorization Failed"**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser
- Ensure Instagram account is not private
- Check Instagram isn't experiencing outages

**"Invalid Credentials"**
- Double-check your Instagram username/password
- Disable two-factor authentication temporarily
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting

**"Insufficient Permissions"**
- Make sure you approved all permissions
- Reconnect and accept all permission requests

**"Account Not Eligible"**
- Ensure account is public (not private)
- Account must have at least 5 posts
- Profile must be complete

---

### App Issues

**Q: App is slow or freezing.**

**Solution:**
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check internet connection
4. Try different browser/device
5. Contact support if issue persists

---

**Q: Notifications aren't working.**

**Solution:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Enable all
2. Check browser/device notification permissions
3. Ensure "Do Not Disturb" is off
4. Re-login to app

---

**Q: Location services not working.**

**Solution:**
1. Check device location permissions
2. Enable location in browser settings
3. Ensure GPS is turned on
4. Try refreshing the app
