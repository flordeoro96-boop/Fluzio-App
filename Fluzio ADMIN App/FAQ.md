## For Customers

### Account & Profile

**Q: Is Fluzio free to use?**  
**A:** Yes! Fluzio is 100% free for customers. You earn points for completing missions and redeem them for rewards. There are no subscription fees or hidden costs.

---

**Q: How do I change my profile picture?**  
**A:** Tap your profile photo → Edit Profile → Tap the camera icon → Select new photo → Save Changes.

---

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
**A:** Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. **Warning:** This is permanent and you'll lose all your points and history.

---

**Q: How do I change my password?**  
**A:** Settings → Security → Change Password → Enter current password → Enter new password → Confirm.

---

**Q: My location is wrong. How do I fix it?**  
**A:** 
1. Enable location services in your device settings
2. Refresh the Fluzio app
3. Or manually set location: Settings → Profile → Location → Update

---

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**  
**A:** No. Each person can only have one account. Multiple accounts may result in suspension.

---

### Missions & Points

**Q: How do points work?**  
**A:** 
- 1 point ≈ $0.01 USD value
- Earn points by completing missions
- Redeem points for rewards from businesses
- Points never expire!

---

**Q: How long does it take to get approved for a mission?**  
**A:** Most businesses review submissions within 24-48 hours. Some may take up to 5 days. You'll receive a notification when reviewed.

---

**Q: What happens if my submission is rejected?**  
**A:** 
1. You'll receive a notification with the reason
2. Read the rejection reason carefully
3. Fix the issue (e.g., add missing hashtag)
4. Resubmit if the mission allows resubmissions
5. No points are deducted for rejections

---

**Q: Can I cancel a mission after applying?**  
**A:** Yes, before you submit proof. Go to My Missions → Active → Select mission → Cancel Application. After submitting proof, you cannot cancel.

---

**Q: How many missions can I do at once?**  
**A:** Unlimited! You can apply for and complete as many missions as you want simultaneously.

---

**Q: Do points expire?**  
**A:** No! Your points stay in your wallet forever.

---

**Q: What's the fastest way to earn points?**  
**A:** 
1. **Daily Login** - 10 points per day (+ bonuses at 7, 30, 100 days)
2. **Social Media Missions** - 100-500 points per mission
3. **Complete Your Profile** - 50 bonus points
4. **Connect Instagram** - 25 bonus points
5. **Refer Friends** - 100 points per referral

---

**Q: Can I transfer points to another user?**  
**A:** Not currently. This feature may be added in the future.

---

**Q: Why was I rejected from applying to a mission?**  
**A:** Common reasons:
- Mission is full (max participants reached)
- You don't meet requirements (e.g., min. followers)
- Your account is too new (needs verification)
- Location requirement (you're too far away)

---

### Rewards & Redemptions

**Q: How do I redeem a reward?**  
**A:** 
1. Go to Wallet → Browse Rewards
2. Select a reward you want
3. Tap "Redeem Now"
4. Confirm points deduction
5. Show QR code at business or use redemption code

---

**Q: Do rewards expire?**  
**A:** It depends on the business. Most rewards expire 30 days after redemption. Check the reward details before redeeming.

---

**Q: Can I get a refund on a redeemed reward?**  
**A:** Generally no, unless:
- Business is closed/unavailable
- Reward was incorrectly described
- Technical error occurred
Contact support for case-by-case review.

---

**Q: The business won't accept my reward. What do I do?**  
**A:** 
1. Show them the QR code in the app
2. Verify the reward hasn't expired
3. Ask to speak with a manager
4. Contact Fluzio support if unresolved

---

**Q: Can I use multiple rewards at once?**  
**A:** It depends on the business's terms. Check the reward description. Most businesses allow one reward per transaction.

---

### Social Media

**Q: Do I need Instagram to use Fluzio?**  
**A:** No, but connecting Instagram unlocks many high-paying social media missions. You can still complete non-Instagram missions.

---

**Q: What information does Fluzio access from my Instagram?**  
**A:** 
- Basic profile info (username, profile photo)
- Public posts (to verify mission completion)
- **We NEVER:**
  - Post on your behalf
  - Access your DMs
  - Store your password
  - Access private information

---

**Q: Can I disconnect Instagram later?**  
**A:** Yes, anytime. Settings → Linked Accounts → Disconnect Instagram. You'll no longer be eligible for Instagram missions.

---

**Q: My Instagram account is private. Can I still do missions?**  
**A:** Most missions require public accounts so businesses can verify your post. You may need to temporarily make your account public or skip Instagram missions.

---

**Q: What if I don't have many followers?**  
**A:** Many missions have low or no follower requirements! Start with those, build your streak, and work up to higher-paying missions.

---

**Q: Can I delete the post after getting approved?**  
**A:** This violates mission terms. Businesses may report you, leading to points deduction or account suspension. Posts should stay up for at least 30 days unless stated otherwise.

---

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app is slow or freezing.**  
**A:** 
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Clear browser cache (Settings → Privacy → Clear Cache)
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
4. Check your internet connection
5. Restart your device

---

**Q: I'm not receiving notifications.**  
**A:** 
1. Check app settings: Settings → Notifications → Enable all
2. Check device/browser notification permissions
3. Ensure "Do Not Disturb" is off
4. Re-login to the app
5. Clear cache and refresh

---

**Q: Pictures won't upload.**  
**A:** 
- Check file size (max 10 MB)
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, HEIC
- Check internet connection
- Try different photo
- Compress large images

---

**Q: Location services not working.**  
**A:** 
1. Enable location in device settings
2. Allow location access for your browser
3. Turn GPS on
4. Refresh the app
5. Try manually entering your location

---

**Q: I forgot my password.**  
**A:** 
1. Go to login screen
2. Tap "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Click link and create new password

---

**Q: My points balance is incorrect.**  
**A:** 
1. Check Transaction History for all activity
2. Look for pending points (missions in review)
3. Check for refunds (cancelled missions)
4. Refresh the app
5. Contact support with specific details

- **SILVER** - $49/month (100 participants max)
- **GOLD** - $149/month (500 participants max)
- **PLATINUM** - $399/month (1,000 participants max + premium features)

**Q: Can I try Fluzio before subscribing?**  
**A:** Yes! Start with the FREE plan. Create missions with up to 50 participants and upgrade anytime.

---

**Q: How do I get verified as a business?**  
**A:** 
1. Complete your business profile
2. Add business address and phone number
3. Verify email address
4. Upload business license (optional but recommended)
5. Verification typically takes 24-48 hours

---

**Q: Can I manage multiple locations?**  
**A:** Yes! Platinum plan includes multi-location management. Contact support to set up additional locations.

---

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
**A:** Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), debit card, and PayPal.

### Mission Management

**Q: How many missions can I run at once?**  
**A:** 
- FREE: 3 active missions
- SILVER: 10 active missions
- GOLD: 25 active missions
- PLATINUM: Unlimited active missions

---

**Q: What's a good points reward amount?**  
**A:** 
- **Instagram Story** - 50-150 points
- **Instagram Post** - 150-300 points
- **Instagram Reel** - 300-500 points
- **Visit + Post** - 200-400 points
- **Review** - 100-200 points

Higher points = more applicants and higher-quality creators.

---

**Q: Can I edit a mission after it's live?**  
**A:** Limited edits only:
- ✅ Can edit: Description, deadline (extend only), max participants (increase only)
- ❌ Cannot edit: Points reward, requirements, proof type

---

**Q: What happens if I cancel a mission?**  
**A:** 
- Mission immediately closes
- All active participants receive full points refund
- Mission shows as "Cancelled" in history
- Cannot be reactivated

---

**Q: How do I handle low-quality submissions?**  
**A:** Reject with a clear reason. Examples:
- "Missing required hashtag #YourBrand"
- "Photo doesn't show our product"
- "Content doesn't match requirements"

Rejections teach participants your standards.

---

**Q: Should I use auto-approval?**  
**A:** 
- **Yes if:** Simple missions, clear requirements, high volume
- **No if:** Brand reputation critical, complex requirements, quality control needed

Test both approaches and see what works.

### Billing & Subscriptions

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?**  
**A:** Yes! Cancel anytime. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

---

**Q: What happens if I downgrade my plan?**  
**A:** 
- Changes take effect at next billing cycle
- Active missions beyond new limit will complete but you can't create new ones until below limit
- No refund for unused portion of current month

---

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
**A:** 
- 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers
- After 30 days, no refunds but you can cancel anytime
- Contact support for special circumstances

---

**Q: How do I get an invoice?**  
**A:** Settings → Billing → View Invoices → Download. Invoices are automatically emailed each billing cycle.

---

**Q: Can I pay annually for a discount?**  
**A:** Yes! Annual plans save 15%:
- SILVER: $499/year (save $89)
- GOLD: $1,499/year (save $289)
- PLATINUM: $4,099/year (save $689)

Contact support to switch to annual billing.

### Analytics & Performance

**Q: Why am I not getting applications?**  
**A:** Common reasons:
- Points reward too low
- Requirements too complex
- Unclear description
- Mission not shared on social media
- Location radius too narrow

**Solution:** Increase points, simplify requirements, promote mission.

---

**Q: What's a good conversion rate?**  
**A:** 
- **Applications → Completions:** 60-80% is good
- **Views → Applications:** 5-15% is typical
- **Submissions → Approvals:** 85%+ is healthy

Below these benchmarks? Simplify requirements or clarify instructions.

---

**Q: How do I export analytics data?**  
**A:** Analytics Dashboard → Select time period → Export to CSV → Download file.

---

**Q: Can I track which missions drive the most revenue?**  
**A:** Yes! Analytics Dashboard shows:
- Mission performance metrics
- Estimated reach and impressions
- Customer acquisition cost
- Revenue attribution (requires integration setup)

---

**Q: What's the difference between views and applications?**  
**A:** 
- **Views** - Mission page was viewed
- **Applications** - User clicked "Apply Now" and joined mission
