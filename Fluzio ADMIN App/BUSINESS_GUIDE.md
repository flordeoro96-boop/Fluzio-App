## Managing Missions

### Mission States

**üü¢ Active** - Currently running, accepting applications
- Edit details (limited changes)
- Pause mission temporarily
- Cancel mission (refunds participants)

**üü° Paused** - Temporarily disabled
- No new applications accepted
- Existing participants can still complete
- Resume anytime

**üî¥ Completed** - Ended successfully
- All participants finished or deadline passed
- View final analytics
- Cannot be reactivated

**‚ö´ Cancelled** - Stopped by business
- All active participants receive full refund
- Cannot be reactivated
- Shows in mission history

### Editing Active Missions

**Can Edit:**
- Description text
- Deadline (extend only, not shorten)
- Max participants (increase only)

**Cannot Edit:**
- Points reward (affects existing participants)
- Proof type
- Post requirements (hashtags, mentions)

**How to Edit:**
1. Go to Missions ‚Üí Active
2. Click mission card
3. Click "Edit Mission"
4. Make changes
5. Save (participants are notified of changes)

### Cancelling Missions

**When to Cancel:**
- Mission no longer needed
- Business closure or emergency
- Incorrect mission details

**Process:**
1. Go to mission details
2. Click "Cancel Mission"
3. Choose refund option:
   - **Full Refund** - Recommended (participants get all points)
   - **Partial Refund** - Custom amount
4. Provide reason (shown to participants)
5. Confirm cancellation

**What Happens:**
- Mission immediately closes
- Active participants notified
- Points automatically refunded
- Mission marked as cancelled

## Reviewing Submissions

### Submission Review Queue

**Pending Submissions:**
- Listed in order received (oldest first)
- Shows participant info, submission proof
- Display submission date/time

**Review Each Submission:**

1. **View Proof**
   - Click to enlarge screenshot/photo
   - Verify link (if applicable)
   - Check content quality

2. **Verify Requirements**
   - ‚úÖ Contains required hashtags
   - ‚úÖ Mentions your business account
   - ‚úÖ Shows required content
   - ‚úÖ Matches post type (story/post/reel)
   - ‚úÖ Submitted on time

3. **Make Decision**

   **‚úÖ APPROVE:**
   - Click "Approve"
   - Points automatically credited to user
   - User receives approval notification
   - Submission marked as complete

   **‚ùå REJECT:**
   - Click "Reject"
   - **Must provide reason** (required)
   - Common reasons:
     - "Missing required hashtag: #YourBrand"
     - "Did not mention @YourAccount"
     - "Content doesn't match requirements"
     - "Submitted after deadline"
   - User can see reason and resubmit (if allowed)

### Review Guidelines

**Approval Criteria:**

‚úÖ **Approve if:**
- All requirements clearly met
- High-quality content
- Submitted on time
- Follows content guidelines

‚ùå **Reject if:**
- Missing hashtags or mentions
- Wrong post type (post instead of story)
- Poor quality or inappropriate content
- Submitted after deadline
- Doesn't show required elements

‚ö†Ô∏è **Gray Areas:**
- Minor hashtag variation (#YourBrand vs #your_brand) - **Approve**
- Typo in mention - Contact user, allow resubmission
- Borderline quality - Consider approving with note
- Partial completion - Reject with clear explanation

### Bulk Actions

**Review Multiple Submissions:**
1. Select submissions (checkbox)
2. Choose bulk action:
   - Approve selected
   - Reject selected (must provide reason)
3. Confirm action

**Use Cases:**
- Approve 10 clearly valid submissions at once
- Reject duplicates or spam

### Response Time

**Best Practices:**
- Review within **24-48 hours**
- Faster reviews = happier customers
- Auto-approval after 7 days (if enabled)

**Automatic Approval:**
- Enable in Mission Settings
- Submissions auto-approve after 3 days if not reviewed
- Reduces workload for high-volume missions

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Q: Not getting enough mission applications.**

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Increase points reward (be competitive)
- ‚úÖ Simplify requirements (remove barriers)
- ‚úÖ Extend deadline (give more time)
- ‚úÖ Improve mission title/description (be exciting)

**Q: Too many applications, can't review them all.**

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Enable auto-approval (Settings)
- ‚úÖ Reduce max participants for future missions
- ‚úÖ Set aside dedicated review time daily
- ‚úÖ Use bulk actions for obvious approvals
- ‚úÖ Increase team capacity (add team members)

---

**Q: Submissions are low quality.**

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Add detailed content guidelines
- ‚úÖ Provide example submissions
- ‚úÖ Set minimum follower count
- ‚úÖ Increase points to attract better creators
- ‚úÖ Be specific about what you want to see
- ‚úÖ Reject low quality (sets standards)

---

**Q: Participants not reading requirements.**

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Bold or CAPITALIZE key requirements
- ‚úÖ Use bullet points for clarity
- ‚úÖ Keep description concise (under 300 words)
- ‚úÖ Include hashtags and mentions at top
- ‚úÖ Send reminder message before deadline

---

**Q: Analytics showing low conversion rate.**

**Analysis:**
- High views, low applications = Requirements too difficult
- High applications, low completions = Unclear instructions
- High submissions, low approvals = Requirements not specific enough

**Solutions:**
- Simplify mission requirements
- Clarify instructions in description
- Provide examples and templates
- Adjust points reward to match effort

---

**Q: Rewards not being redeemed.**

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Lower points cost (make more affordable)
- ‚úÖ Offer more valuable rewards
- ‚úÖ Promote rewards in missions
- ‚úÖ Create limited-time exclusive rewards
- ‚úÖ Survey customers about desired rewards
